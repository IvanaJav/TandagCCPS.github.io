Tandag Component City Police Station

The Tandag Component City Police Station, part of the Philippine National Police, maintains peace and order in Tandag, Surigao del Sur. focusing on crime prevention, law enforcement, and community safety through patrolling, investigations, and outreach programs.

Focusing On What Matters Most

The Tandag Component City Police Station (TCCPS) in Tandag City, Philippines, provides a range of services aimed at maintaining peace and order, ensuring public safety, and enforcing laws within its jurisdiction.

Public safety programs are a significant aspect of our services, with initiatives aimed at educating the public on crime prevention, conducting safety drills, and implementing anti-drug and anti-violence campaigns.
